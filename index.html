About Us

We’re Mooth Terry, an improv troupe devoted to big swings, committed nonsense, and making an audience believe that three fully grown adults absolutely are running a lighthouse-themed divorce law firm.

We like fast laughs, weird worlds, and the kind of scenes that begin with one suggestion and end in emotional chaos.
