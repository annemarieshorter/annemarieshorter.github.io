About Us

Moose Terry is a Metro Detroit improv troupe forged in the fires of local improv class signups, sustained by commitment, and powered by the dangerous belief that a room full of adults can make something beautiful out of the word “bologna.”

The group met in January 2025 while studying improv together at Go Comedy in Ferndale, Michigan and has been performing, practicing, and making each other laugh ever since. Their comedy is fast, physical, and deeply willing to follow a terrible idea until it becomes a great one.
